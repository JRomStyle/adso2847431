Straight Outta Compton

The story tells of the rise to success of the group N.W.A (Niggas With Attitudes) and its subsequent decomposition due, fundamentally, to disagreements over contracts and the way of distributing money between them. Later there would be a failed reunion of the group and a new meeting following the death of Eazy-E due to the HIV virus.

Description

N.W.A. (short for Niggaz With Attitude, which in Spanish would be translated as "Blacks with Attitude" or "Blacks with Pride"), was an American hip hop group from Compton, California, and is widely considered the pioneering group in the formation of the gangsta rap musical subgenre.
